5 Day Trip

Kerala, often referred to as "God's Own Country," is a captivating state located in the southwestern region of India. Known for its scenic beauty, cultural heritage, and rich biodiversity, Kerala has become a popular tourist destination worldwide. Whether you seek tranquility in its backwaters, adventure in its wildlife reserves, or a glimpse into its vibrant traditions, Kerala promises an unforgettable journey for every visitor.


Whether you're strolling through the tea gardens of Munnar, cruising along the backwaters of Alleppey, or simply sipping on a freshly brewed cup of local tea, Kerala will weave its magic around you, leaving you with memories to cherish forever.
Come, experience the charm of Kerala, where nature's beauty and heartfelt warmth blend seamlessly to create an unforgettable journey of a lifetime.

Day 1: Arrival in kerala

Upon arrival in Kerala, our representative will meet you at the airport or railway station and escort you to hotel. After some rest, you will be visiting neyyar wildlife sancturay ,later which you can enjoy Kerala's authentic cuisines.



Day 2: Boating

After breakfast, you will be taken for boating and fishing activities at Neyyar Dam ,where you will be swayed by the rich scencic heritage and the food provided by warm and welcoming local villagers will leave you craving for some more time in here.



Day 3: Sky Diving

After visiting AMAS ,which runs various training programs and has the equipment for all sorts of adventure sports. you can experience the most thrilling moments and capture them.Later you can explore the local markets for shopping.


Day 4:Scuba Diving

you will be visiting Kovalam Beach which is a great escape to travelers to rejuvenante in tranqulity.Here you can indulge the beauty of aqua species by diving in the beach which is the thrilling part.



Day 5: A Refreshing Embrace

You will be visting Meenumutty Waterfalls,the trail to this waterfall goes among the dense forests,under the canopy formed by green foliage.Here you can do fun activities like trekking thus you can satiate your craving for adventure.
