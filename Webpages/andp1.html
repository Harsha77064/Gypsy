7 Day Trip

The Andaman Islands are a group of islands located in the Bay of Bengal, forming part of the Andaman and Nicobar Islands - Union Territory of India. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, pristine beaches, diverse wildlife, and rich cultural heritage.

Picture yourself standing on the powdery white sands, the crystal-clear turquoise waters gently kissing your toes. The Andaman Islands boast some of the most picturesque beaches in the world, where time seems to stand still, and worries fade away like the receding waves.Whether you seek adventure or tranquility, the Andaman Islands offer a perfect blend of both.

So, come, let the Andaman Islands weave their magic around you. Let the ocean breeze whisper secrets in your ear and the starlit sky ignite your dreams. Lose yourself in the allure of this remote paradise, and you'll find a piece of heaven on Earth, waiting to be embraced by your heart.

Day 1: Arrival in Andaman

Upon arrival in Andaman, our representative will meet you at the airport by boat they transfer you to your hotel. After some rest, you can visit the famous Beach Port Blair,Havelock Islands,Neil Islands and ending up with Beach Paradise while heading back to hotel you will be visting some extraordinary shops and you can do some shopping following you will be arranged with beach side fresh food for those who are intrested and normal cuisines also available.


Day 2: Visiting Beaches

After breakfast, we will take you to visit the famous Pristine Beaches, one of the most popular attractions in Andaman. The Andaman Islands are renowned for their pristine white sandy beaches and crystal-clear turquoise waters. Radhanagar Beach on Havelock Island has consistently been ranked as one of the best beaches in Asia. The beaches here offer tranquility, serenity, and breathtaking natural beauty. .


Day 3:Scuba Diving

After breakfast, we will depart for Andaman Islands. On arrival, check-in to your hotel and rest for a while. In the evening, you can visit the scuba diving spot and start your diving.The Andaman Islands boast rich and diverse marine life, making it a paradise for snorkelers and scuba divers. The surrounding coral reefs are home to a vibrant array of colorful fish, sea turtles, manta rays, and even dugongs (sea cows). Popular dive sites like North Bay, Neil Island, and Mahatma Gandhi Marine National Park provide an opportunity to explore this underwater wonderland.


Day 4: Cellular Jail

After breakfast, we will take you to explore the cellular jail. The Cellular Jail in Port Blair, the capital of the Andaman Islands, holds great historical significance. It was used by the British colonial rulers as a prison to incarcerate freedom fighters during India's struggle for independence. Today, the Cellular Jail stands as a memorial and museum, showcasing the harsh realities of the past and honoring the sacrifices made by the freedom fighters.

VIP will have a VIP Entry to the Forts with a Tourist Guide


Day 5: Adventure Sports

After breakfast, we will depart for Adventure sports, The Andaman Islands offer various adventure sports for thrill-seekers. You can indulge in activities like sea walking, jet skiing, banana boat rides, parasailing, and kayaking. These activities allow visitors to experience the adrenaline rush while enjoying the scenic beauty of the islands.

Day 6: Unspoiled Nature and Ecotourism

After breakfast, we will take you to visit the famous Unspoiled Nature and Ecotourism, A significant portion of the Andaman Islands remains untouched and unspoiled, offering a true escape into nature. The lush green forests, dense mangroves, and tropical wildlife provide a serene environment for nature lovers. There are also opportunities for eco-friendly activities like birdwatching, trekking, and visiting nature reserves like the Mahatma Gandhi Marine National Park and Mount Harriet National Park.

Day 7: Departure

After breakfast, our representative will transfer you to the airport for your onward journey.
