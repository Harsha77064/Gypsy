About Package


5 Day Expedition

The region's unique geographical features, including the mighty Brahmaputra River and the lush green valleys, create a fertile ecosystem that supports a wide range of plant and animal species. Assam is famous for its lush tea gardens, where acres of vibrant green tea bushes carpet the landscape.As you explore the enchanting forests and meandering rivers of Assam, you will be captivated by the symphony of colors, the melodious calls of birds, and the harmonious coexistence of nature's treasures. Assam's unique flora and fauna provide a glimpse into the untamed beauty and ecological significance of this enchanting region.

| Plan |


Day 1: Arrival in Guwahati

Arrive in Guwahati, the gateway to Assam.
Meet and greet with our representative who will assist you with the transfer to Manas National Park.
Check-in to a resort near Manas National Park and relax.
Enjoy the evening at leisure to explore the surroundings.
Overnight stay in Manas.


Day 2: Manas National Park

Embark on an early morning safari in Manas National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its incredible biodiversity.
Explore the park's diverse ecosystems, including grasslands, wetlands, and forests.
Spot wildlife such as the Bengal tiger, Indian rhinoceros, wild elephants, clouded leopards, and numerous bird species.
Visit the nearby Manas Maozigendri Ecotourism Society for insights into local conservation efforts.
Overnight stay in Manas.


Day 3: Nameri National Park

Transfer to Nameri National Park, located on the banks of the Jia Bhoroli River.
Check-in to a resort near Nameri and relax.
Afternoon visit to the Nameri Eco Camp to learn about eco-conservation and take part in nature activities.
Enjoy a peaceful evening stroll along the river or indulge in bird watching.
Overnight stay in Nameri.


Day 4: Nameri ---- Tezpur

Start the day with a thrilling river rafting experience on the Jia Bhoroli River.
Embark on a nature walk or trekking expedition in Nameri National Park to explore its rich flora and fauna.
Proceed to Tezpur, a historic city known for its archaeological sites and lush gardens.
Visit Agnigarh Hill, Cole Park, and Mahabhairav Temple.
Overnight stay in Tezpur.


Day 5: Kaziranga - I

Travel to Kaziranga National Park, one of the most famous wildlife reserves in India.
Check-in to a resort near Kaziranga and relax.
Afternoon jeep safari in the Central Range of Kaziranga to spot the iconic one-horned rhinoceros, elephants, tigers, and other wildlife.
Visit the nearby Panbari Reserve Forest for bird watching and nature walks.
Overnight stay in Kaziranga.


Day 6: Kaziranga - II

An early morning elephant safari in the Western Range of Kaziranga for a unique wildlife viewing experience.
After breakfast, explore the Eastern Range of Kaziranga on a jeep safari to discover more wildlife and enjoy the scenic landscapes.
Visit the Kaziranga Orchid and Biodiversity Park to witness a variety of orchids and other exotic plants.
Overnight stay in Kaziranga.


Day 7: Departure

Return to Guwahati.
Explore the city's attractions, including Kamakhya Temple, Umananda Temple (located on an island in the Brahmaputra River), and Assam State Museum.
If time permits, take a sunset cruise on the Brahmaputra River.
Depart from Guwahati with beautiful memories of your nature-centric trip to Assam.
