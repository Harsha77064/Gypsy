GUWAHATI

Gateway to the NorthEast

Poised on the banks of the Brahamputra River, this rapidly growing city which is the largest metropolis of the North-East provides a link to seven other northeastern states. Its hills hold a 10th-century temple dedicated to the goddess Kamakhya and an ancient seat of astronomy known as Navagraha, the temple of the nine planets; the small Umananda temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, is on an island in the river. The area around the city contains wildlife sanctuaries, archeological ruins and a silk-weaving centre.
