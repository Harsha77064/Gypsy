8 Day Trip

Rajasthan is a beautiful state located in the north-western region of India. It is famous for its rich history, culture, and heritage. The state is dotted with several forts, palaces, and temples that showcase its rich architectural and cultural heritage. From the colorful streets of Jaipur to the serene lakes of Udaipur, Rajasthan has something for everyone.


In the heart of Rajasthan, the spirit of valor, chivalry, and royalty lives on, making it a destination that not only dazzles with its architectural marvels but also leaves an indelible mark on the soul. Discover the charm of Rajasthan, where dreams come to life amidst a world of royal grandeur and magical moments.


| Plan |


Day 1: Arrival in Jaipur

Upon arrival in Jaipur, our representative will meet you at the airport or railway station and transfer you to your hotel. After some rest, you can visit the famous Hawa Mahal, the City Palace, and the Jantar Mantar. In the evening, you can enjoy shopping at the local markets and try some delicious Rajasthani cuisine.


Day 2: Jaipur Sightseeing

Jaipur: After Break Fast Morning excursion to Amber Fort. Make your ascent to the palace fortress on the gaily-caparisoned elephant back (optional). Tour the chamber & hallways of the palace, which are famous for the excellence of their design & decoration. Afternoon tour of Jaipur known as “ The pink city of Rajasthan “ visit the unique Janter Manter observatory, which was built in 1700’s, yet even today still looks futuristic or almost surrealistic: the city palace & its museum, the Hawa Mahal or the palace of winds. Night Stay at Hotel Teej.


Day 3: Jaipur to Bikaner

Jaipur - Bikaner: After Breakfast drive to Bikaner to arrive there by afternoon and check in, the Hotel Dhola Maru for a Night stay.


Day 4: Bikaner Junagarh Fort and Museum Sightseeing

Bikaner / Jaisalmer: After Breakfast drive visit to Junagarh Fort and Museum. After lunch leave for Jaisalmer to arrive in the evening and check-in, in Hotel Moomal Jaisalmer. Night Stay at Hotel.


Day 5: Jaisalmer Sightseeing

Jaisalmer:In the morning sightseeing of Fort, Jain Temples, Havelis, and Gadhisar Lake. In the afternoon visit Lodurva Jain temples and drive to Sam sand dunes to have a camel ride at sunset point. Night Stay at Hotel Moomal.


Day 6: Jodhpur Sightseeing

Jaisalmer - Jodhpur: After breakfast drive to Jodhpur en route to visit the temples of Osian. Check-in, in Hotel Ghoomar, Jodhpur for Night Stay.


Day 7: Jodhpur -- Pushkar

Jodhpur - Pushkar: After Breakfast drive a visit to Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada, and Umaid Bhawan Palace. After Lunch drive to Pushkar. Arrive in the Evening and check-in, in at Hotel Sarovar, Pushkar. In the evening visit to Brahma temple and ghats. Perform the ceremony of Deep Dan at the Sarovar Lake. Night Stay at Hotel Sarovar.


Day 8: Departure

Pushkar - Jaipur:After Breakfast drive to Jaipur, enroute visit to Khwaja Dargah and Adhai Din ka Jhonpra at Ajmer. Arrive by lunchtime in Jaipur and departure for the onward destination Airport/ Railway Station/ Bus Station and Tour Ends. Packed lunch would be provided en route.
