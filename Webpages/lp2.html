6 Day Biking Adventure

A vacation to Leh Ladakh in India opens up the sceninc views of snow-capped hills and mountains, towering peaks, risky roads and screaming motorcycle engines.
In a nutshell, embarking on a Ladakh bike tour brings in a barrage of positive feelings for every rider.

Are you excited to travel to Leh this season?
Let's take a look at the thrilling tour we have curated for you!


| Plan |


Day 1 : Arrival in Leh

Our services start with your arrival at the Leh airport. We will transfer you to the Hotel and you'd be given a complete day for rest which is important for acclimatization to the rarified air. Visit the Leh Bazaar and explore the local surroundings in the evening. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.



Day 2 : Leh

After breakfast we leave to visit the famous Magnetic Point, Gurudwara Pathar Sahib and The Hall of fame. We also visit the Sangam which is the confluence of the Zanskar and the Indus river. Dinner and overnight stay at the Hotel.



Day 3 : Leh -- Nubra Valley

After breakfast, we would start our ride to Nubra Valley (120 Kms) via Khardungla (Highest accessible Motorable road in the World, 18,380 ft). Arrive at Deskit by Afternoon. Rest of the day is free for you to explore Deskit, Hunder Village and enjoy camel safari at the Sand Dunes between Deskit and Hunder Village. Overnight stay at the campsite.



Day 4 : Nubra Valley -- Pangong Tso

After enjoying the beauty and captivating views of the Nubra Valley, we get ready to check another location that would leave us awestruck off our list - the Pangong Lake! We begin our ride post breakfast towards the ink blue lake, taking the unexplored and untouched Shyok village route. This route is famous for its waterfalls, and the beautiful scenic view. The first view of the lake will leave you spellbound and as we breathe that in, we continue towards our campsite near the lake. A sumptuous dinner with a chit-chat session would bring an end to the day.



Day 5 : Pangong Tso -- Leh

After breakfast, we will ride back to Leh, crossing Changla Pass 17,350 ft. (third Highest Motorable road in the world). The ride will leave you spell bound with the beautiful views and mind blowing landscape. We retreat to the hotel for a night stay. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.



Day 6 : Departure

In the morning post breakfast, we leave for the Kushok Bakula Rimpochee Airport, Leh and bring to an end a beautiful trip taking with us memories worth a lifetime and friendships to cherish forever!
