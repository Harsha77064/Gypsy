6 Day Trip

The Andaman Islands are a group of islands located in the Bay of Bengal, forming part of the Andaman and Nicobar Islands - Union Territory of India. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, pristine beaches, diverse wildlife, and rich cultural heritage.

Picture yourself standing on the powdery white sands, the crystal-clear turquoise waters gently kissing your toes. The Andaman Islands boast some of the most picturesque beaches in the world, where time seems to stand still, and worries fade away like the receding waves.

Whether you seek adventure or tranquility, the Andaman Islands offer a perfect blend of both. Explore hidden caves, trek through dense jungles, or simply bask in the serenity of the setting sun. These islands hold the promise of unforgettable memories, etching themselves into the core of your being.


So, come, let the Andaman Islands weave their magic around you. Let the ocean breeze whisper secrets in your ear and the starlit sky ignite your dreams. Lose yourself in the allure of this remote paradise, and you'll find a piece of heaven on Earth, waiting to be embraced by your heart.

Day 1: Port Blair

Upon arrival in Andaman, our representative will meet you at the airport and transfer you to your hotel. After some rest, you can visit the famous Port Blair, The capital city and the entry point to the islands. Visit Cellular Jail, Samudrika Naval Marine Museum, and enjoy the picturesque views from Corbyn's Cove Beach.



Day 2: Havelock Island

After breakfast, we will depart for Havelock Island . On arrival, check in to your hotel and rest for a while. After breakfast, you will be picked up from the hotel for a day tour of Havelock Island. Dinner at the rooftop - barbecue platter. Overnight stay at the hotel. followed by a visit to the local beach side markets.




Day 3: Neil Island

After breakfast, leave for Neil Island in a sedan from the hotel. On arrival, you will be served a welcome drink at the hotel. Check in to the hotel and have lunch hotel. In the afternoon, local sightseeing of beaches in islands.. In the evening, enjoy a swim at the pool. Dinner & overnight stay at the hotel.



Day 4: Long Island

You will be picked up early in the morning from the hotel for Long Island. A serene and less crowded island offering beautiful beaches and mangrove creeks. Enjoy nature walks and birdwatching here. Boating and sightseeing at the park return to the hotel.Dinner & overnight stay at the hotel.



Day 5: Barren Island

After breakfast, we will take you to visit the Barren Island, If you're interested in volcanoes, visit Barren Island, which is home to India's only active volcano. However, access to the island may be restricted at times.

Day 6: Departure

After breakfast, our representative will transfer you to the airport for your onward journey. complimentary drinks and snacks would be provided en route.
