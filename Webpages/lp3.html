11 Day Tempo Travel

Leh Ladakh can be best described as India’s Shangri La. A land that looks too distant to be true, it is wedged between the high Himalayas. Dotted by high-altitude lakes and lofty peaks such as Stok Kangri, Leh and Ladakh offer an experience that does not have an equal. While stargazing might just be the most fascinating thing to do here, driving through the high mountain passes and wandering in the winding lanes of Leh are once-in-a-lifetime experiences. It is not without any reason that the Leh Ladakh trip is rated as one of the most sought-after road trips on Earth.


| Plan |


As you land at the Srinagar airport and reach the hotel by yourself, our team members will meet you at the Deluxe Houseboat or Hotel at Dal Lake. After lunch, enjoy a Shikara ride on the lake and proceed towards the famous Mughal Garden. This also includes a visit to Nishat Bagh and Shalimar Gardens and back for sightseeing.


Day 02: Srinagar to Kargil

In the morning, leave for Kargil after breakfast, en route visiting Sonamarg, Zojila pass, Dras Village (2nd Coldest Inhabited place in the World), War memorial hall & Museum. Reach Kargil by evening. Dinner and Overnight stay in Kargil.


Day 03 : Kargil to Leh

In the morning after breakfast leave for Leh. En route visit Lamayuru Monastery, Magnetic Hill, Pathar Sahib Gurudwara and the Hall of Fame museum. Dinner and overnight stay in Leh.



Day 04: A free day for exploring (ME TIME)

Today is a free day and you can explore the local market, acclimatize properly and enjoy me-time. Dinner and overnight stay in Leh.



Day 05 : Leh to Nubra Valley

After b


reakfast, drive to Nubra Valley via Khardungla (The highest Motorable road in the World, 18,380 ft). Post-lunch, enjoy the camel safari at Sand Dunes between Diskit & Hundur Village. Dinner and Overnight stay in Nubra Valley.


Day 06 : Nubra – Turtuk – Nubra

It is one of the most fascinating journeys in Ladakh. This is a less popular destination that recently opened for tourists. If you have a thirst for a beautiful location this is the place. A border village that was recaptured by the Indian Army in 1971, this region is controlled mainly by the Ladakh Scouts, a rear department of the Indian Army. After a full day of rough excursion, you will come back to Nubra Valley by the evening. Overnight stay at the camp in Nubra Valley.


Day 07: Nubra Valley to Pangong Tso via Shyok route

After breakfast, visit Diskit Monastery (Big Buddha), where you can have a magnificent view of the Nubra Valley and later drive to Pangong via the Shyok River with amazing landscapes and adventure. Stop for lunch at Durbuk and 1hrs continue to drive to Pangong Lake. Enjoy the overnight stay and dinner near Pangong Tso.



Day 08 : Pangong Tso to Leh via Changla

In the early morning, enjoy the picturesque view of Sunrise on the lake. After Breakfast, drive back to Leh; en-route visit Thiksey Monastery and Shey Palace. Our driver will drop you at Leh Market where you can explore yourself. Dinner and overnight stay in Leh.



Day 09: Leh to Jispa via Sarchu

After breakfast, drive to Sarchu passing Skyangchu Thang. Enjoy your lunch at Pang, by crossing Nakeela and Lachlung La. Continue to drive to Jispa. Arrive at Jispa by the evening. Dinner and overnight stay at Jispa.


Day 10: Jispa to Manali

After breakfast, start the journey to Manali. A beautiful scenic drive crossing Baralacha La (4845m), the route will leave you in a state of wonder. Lunch at Sissu village. Later, cross the famous Rohtang Pass, 13050 ft. before reaching Manali and checking into the hotel. Dinner and overnight stay in Manali.


Day 11: Manali tour end (Departure)

Today, half day Sightseeing at Hadimba temple, Vashisht temple, local market, Solang Valley and drop to the Market. The wonderful tour of Ladakh ends here. We leave with a bagful of memories and friendships to last a lifetime
