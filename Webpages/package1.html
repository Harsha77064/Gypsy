5 Day Expedition

Poised on the banks of the Brahamputra River, this rapidly growing city which is the largest metropolis of the North-East provides a link to seven other northeastern states. Its hills hold a 10th-century temple dedicated to the goddess Kamakhya and an ancient seat of astronomy known as Navagraha, the temple of the nine planets; the small Umananda temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, is on an island in the river. The area around the city contains wildlife sanctuaries, archeological ruins and a silk-weaving center.

| Plan |


Day 1: Guwahati ----- Shillong

Arrive at Guwahati and transfer to Shillong.
Visit Umium Lake, a majestic placid lake that lies en route.On arrival at Shillong, check in to your hotel and settle in.
Evening free for leisure.
Overnight stay at the hotel in Shillong.


Day 2: Excursion to Cherrapunji

Get up early today to enjoy the mesmerizing mornings of Shillong.
Embark on a trekking expedition to the picturesque hills of Cherrapunjee, known for its stunning waterfalls and living root bridges.
After an early breakfast, drive to Cherrapunji, one of the wettest places in the world.
Visit Eco Park, Dainthlen Falls, Nohkalikai Falls, Nohsngithiang Falls (Seven Sisters Falls), Mawsmai Cave, and Thangkharang Park.
Evening return to Shillong.
Visit Elephanta Falls and Shillong Peak for some breathtaking views.
Overnight stay in Shillong.

VIP tourists will have a guided visit to the inner cohorts of the hills and caves individually.


Day 3: Shillong ---- Bhalukpong

After breakfast, leave for Bhalukpong
On reaching Bhalupkung, check in at your hotel.
Visit the Bhalukpong Fort
Take a tour of Tipi today. View more than 50000 varieties of orchids.
Return back to the hotel for an overnight stay at the hotel.

VIP pass includes a visit to the National Orchid Research Centre followed by detailed tour of the agri-techniques.


Day 4: Bhalukpong ---- Kaziranga

After breakfast, leave for Kaziranga.
Check in to your room.
Evening free for leisure.
Overnight stay at the hotel in Kaziranga.
Explore any one zone of Kaziranga National Park on the back of an elephant early morning.Enjoy the thrilling experience of watching the wildlife.
Return to the resort and have breakfast.
Evening free for leisure.
Leave for Guwahati at night.


Day 5: Departure

Check in to your hotel.
Morning you may take a River Cruise (Direct Payment) on the mighty River Brahmaputra.
Enjoy river rafting on the mighty Brahmaputra River and go for a thrilling zip-lining experience in the nearby adventure park.
You may also visit the local market which is famous for Assam Silk, particularly Golden Muga Silk, Assam Tea, Bamboo and Cane Products.
Check out from the hotel in the late evening.
With everlasting memories, transfer to Guwahati airport/railway station for an onward journey.
