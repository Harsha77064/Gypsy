About Package


7 Day Trip

Kerala, often referred to as "God's Own Country", is a captivating state located in the southwestern region of India. Known for its scenic beauty, cultural heritage, and rich biodiversity, Kerala has become a popular tourist destination worldwide.

Whether you seek tranquility in its backwaters, adventure in its wildlife reserves, or a glimpse into its vibrant traditions, Kerala promises an unforgettable journey for every visitor.

Whether you're strolling through the tea gardens of Munnar, cruising along the backwaters of Alleppey, or simply sipping on a freshly brewed cup of local tea, Kerala will weave its magic around you, leaving you with memories to cherish forever.

Come, experience the charm of Kerala, where nature's beauty and heartfelt warmth blend seamlessly to create an unforgettable journey of a lifetime.

| Plan |


Day 1: Arrival in Cochin

Meet and greet with our representative and transfer to your hotel. Enjoy a leisurely evening exploring the local markets and cuisine. Overnight stay in Cochin.




Day 2: sight seeing

Visit the famous Mattancherry Palace,explore the historic Jewish Synagogue in the heart of the Jew Town. Witness the iconic Chinese Fishing Nets along the coastline. Enjoy a Kathakali dance performance in the evening




Day 3: Munnar Hill Station

Depart for Munnar, a picturesque hill station known for its tea plantations. visit the Cheeyappara and Valara waterfalls. Overnight stay in Munnar.



Day 4:Munnar Exploration

Visit the Eravikulam National Park, home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr,explore the Tea Museum to learn about tea processing. Enjoy a boat ride in the serene Mattupetty Dam. Overnight stay in Munnar.




Day 5: Alleppey Backwaters

Drive to Alleppey and board a traditional houseboat for a backwater cruise. Enjoy delicious Kerala cuisine prepared onboard by the chef. Overnight stay on the houseboat.




Day 6: Alleppey to Kumarakom

Check-in to a beautiful resort amidst the backwaters. Relax and enjoy Ayurvedic spa treatments to rejuvenate your mind and body. Overnight stay in Kumarakom.




Day 7: Kumarakom Ayurveda Tour

Continue your Ayurveda wellness tour with personalized treatments. Consult with Ayurveda experts and undergo therapeutic massages. Explore the lush bird sanctuary in Kumarakom.
