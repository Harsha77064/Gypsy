6 Day Trip

The Andaman Islands are a group of islands located in the Bay of Bengal, forming part of the Andaman and Nicobar Islands - Union Territory of India. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, pristine beaches, diverse wildlife, and rich cultural heritage.

Picture yourself standing on the powdery white sands, the crystal-clear turquoise waters gently kissing your toes. The Andaman Islands boast some of the most picturesque beaches in the world, where time seems to stand still, and worries fade away like the receding waves.

Whether you seek adventure or tranquility, the Andaman Islands offer a perfect blend of both. Explore hidden caves, trek through dense jungles, or simply bask in the serenity of the setting sun. These islands hold the promise of unforgettable memories, etching themselves into the core of your being.


So, come, let the Andaman Islands weave their magic around you. Let the ocean breeze whisper secrets in your ear and the starlit sky ignite your dreams. Lose yourself in the allure of this remote paradise, and you'll find a piece of heaven on Earth, waiting to be embraced by your heart.

Day 1: Elephant Beach

Upon arrival in Andaman, our representative will meet you at the airport and transfer you to your hotel. After some rest, you can visit the famous Elephant Beach, Situated on Havelock Island, Elephant Beach is known for its coral reefs and vibrant marine life. You can enjoy snorkeling, swimming, and a delightful picnic on the beach.



Day 2: Corbyn's Cove Beach

After Break Fast Morning excursion to Corbyn's Cove Beach. Located near Port Blair, Corbyn's Cove Beach is a popular tourist spot. The beach offers serene surroundings and beautiful views, making it an ideal spot for a picnic.



Day 3: Wandoor Beach

After Breakfast drive to Wandoor Beach to arrive there by afternoon and check in, the Hotel Dhola Maru for a Night stay.

VIP will have a luxurious A/C rooms with well server room service at there door steps


Day 4: Laxmanpur Beach

After Breakfast drive visit to Laxmanpur Beach. After lunch leave for Laxmanpur Beach hotel to arrive in the evening and check-in, in Hotel Moomal Jaisalmer. Night Stay at Hotel.



Day 5: Chidiya Tapu

In the morning sightseeing of Chidiya Tapu, Known as the "Bird Island," Chidiya Tapu is a haven for birdwatchers and nature enthusiasts. It offers spectacular views, a forest trail, and a serene beach where you can have a peaceful picnic amidst nature.

Day 6: North Bay Island

After breakfast drive to North Bay Island en route to visit the Island. Check-in, in Hotel.Also known as Coral Island, North Bay Island offers excellent opportunities for snorkeling and scuba diving. You can enjoy a picnic on the beach while marveling at the vibrant coral reefs.



Day 6: Departure

After breakfast, our representative will transfer you to the airport or railway station for your onward journey. Packed lunch would be provided en route.
