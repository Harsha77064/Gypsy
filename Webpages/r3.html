6 Day Trip

Rajasthan is a beautiful state located in the north-western region of India. It is famous for its rich history, culture, and heritage. The state is dotted with several forts, palaces, and temples that showcase its rich architectural and cultural heritage. From the colorful streets of Jaipur to the serene lakes of Udaipur, Rajasthan has something for everyone.


In the heart of Rajasthan, the spirit of valor, chivalry, and royalty lives on, making it a destination that not only dazzles with its architectural marvels but also leaves an indelible mark on the soul. Discover the charm of Rajasthan, where dreams come to life amidst a world of royal grandeur and magical moments.


| Plan |


Day 1: Arrival in Jaipur

Upon arrival in Jaipur, our representative will meet you at the airport or railway station and transfer you to your hotel. After some rest, you can visit the famous Hawa Mahal, the City Palace, and the Jantar Mantar. In the evening, you can enjoy shopping at the local markets and try some delicious Rajasthani cuisine.


Day 2: Jaipur Sightseeing

After breakfast, we will depart for Jaipur. On arrival, check in to your hotel and rest for a while. After breakfast, you will be picked up from the hotel for a day tour of Jaipur. Dinner at the rooftop - barbecue platter. Overnight stay at the hotel. followed by a visit to the local markets.


Day 3: Jaipur to Ranthambore

After breakfast, leave for Ranthambore in a sedan from the hotel. On arrival, you will be served a welcome drink at the hotel. Check in to the hotel and have lunch hotel. In the afternoon, local sightseeing of forts, museums, and temples. In the evening, enjoy a swim at the pool. Dinner & overnight stay at the hotel.


Day 4: Safari at Ranthambhore

You will be picked up early in the morning from the hotel for the safari. Enjoy the safari. After the safari, you will be picked up from the national park gate and will be dropped at the hotel.After breakfast, leave for the crocodile park. Boating and sightseeing at the park return to the hotel.Dinner & overnight stay at the hotel.


Day 5: Udaipur Sightseeing

After breakfast, we will take you to visit the famous City Palace, one of the largest royal complexes in Rajasthan. You can also visit the Jagdish Temple and the Saheliyon Ki Bari. In the evening, you can explore the local markets and try some delicious Rajasthani food.


Day 6: Departure

After breakfast, our representative will transfer you to the airport or railway station for your onward journey. Packed lunch would be provided en route.
